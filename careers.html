CA Articleship Training

2-Year Structured Programme · Nagpur HQ

Under the new ICAI guidelines (effective 2024), the articleship period is 2 years. At VKS, your training is structured across all key practice areas under direct partner mentorship.

Rotational Training — Audit, Direct Tax, GST, Legal & Advisory
Partner Mentorship — Direct guidance from founding partners
Diverse Clients — Listed companies, MNCs, MSMEs & PSUs
GMCS & ITT — Supported and coordinated by the firm
Stipend — As per ICAI norms · Performance-linked increments
Placement Assistance — Top performers offered full-time roles

Eligibility: CA Foundation / Intermediate cleared. ICAI registration required. Please attach your mark sheets and ICAI registration details.
